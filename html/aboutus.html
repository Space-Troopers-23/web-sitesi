    • About Us

    The Space Troopers Rover Team was founded in December 2019 with great excitement, ideals, and goals under the IEEE Hacettepe umbrella. Although our team started during the online period, with its outstanding efforts and discipline demonstrated in two years, we have been designing more innovative and successful models every day and climbing to higher positions in the rankings. With the team culture we have created, we have actually built the path to success, beyond success. Our team aims to serve as an example to curious scientists and engineers who want to participate in ERC and similar competitions by sharing all of our work openly accessible to everyone. In this direction, we adopt growing and developing by sharing as a goal, and aim to provide a solid infrastructure to future generations and teams who will take over this job or come up with new ideas. If you would like to learn more detailed information about our work (mechanical and electronic designs, algorithm schemes, sample software projects, experiment procedures, etc.), you can let us know in your reply email.
